About SmartChef

SmartChef was born out of frustration with food waste and the daily struggle of deciding what to cook. Our mission is to help home cooks make the most of their ingredients, reduce food waste, and discover delicious new recipes.

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by a team of food enthusiasts and tech experts, SmartChef combines culinary expertise with artificial intelligence to solve a common household problem. What started as a personal project to use up leftovers has grown into a platform helping thousands of users worldwide.

How It Works

SmartChef uses advanced algorithms to match your available ingredients with thousands of recipes in our database. When you're missing an ingredient, our smart substitution system suggests alternatives based on flavor profiles, dietary needs, and common pantry staples.
